About Us
Shree Venkateshwara College of Arts and Science, an institution for achievers, located in a serene atmosphere, is situated at Othakuthirai, near Gobichettipalayam. The college was founded by Shree Venkateshwara Educational and Charitable Trust in the year 2019 with the divine aim of educating the students of rural background. It inculcates in them, the technical and innovative skills catering to their needs of the present scenario. The Institution also provides excellent infrastructure in relation to practical. Equipped with state of art, laboratories, workshops and libraries, the institution aims to provide the total gamut of requirements without any lacuna. The Institution has employed dedicated, experienced and empathetic staff to guide and nurture the budding students to become the pillars of a good society. It imparts professionalism and critical thinking in them, . . .
Vision
“To become a premier technical Institute to produce value based competent professionals with scientific temper, team spirit and ethics.”
Mission
To provide good academic ambience for inculcate a culture of continuous learning with appropriate resources and facilities.
To commit the students towards industrial needs through value based education and modern technology.
To provide effective and responsible graduates with social commitment and ethics.
